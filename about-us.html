Al Samar Company is an Emirati company working in the field of foodstuff trading. The company was established in Abu Dhabi in the beginning of July 2022.

The company works to provide the market needs of grains such as wheat, rice and corn, fresh foodstuffs, ration goods and snacks to various outlets and places of providing food services. The company adopts a flexible policy in the places of supplying sales outlets and service places, and this falls under our responsibility to contribute to achieving food security and fulfilling local and global needs of the need for food.
